##Admin commands: /cup `command` _parameter_

###/cup `limit` {number}
    sets new rounds points limit

###/cup `rounds` {mm:ss}
    sets winners number

###/cup `nbwin` {mm:ss}
    sets winners number

###/cup `fto` {mm:ss}
    sets finish timeout

###/cup `wu` {mm:ss}
    sets warmup duration

###/cup `res`
    restart and reset scores

###/cup `skip`
    skip and reset scores
